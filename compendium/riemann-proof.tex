\subsection*{The EFT Thesis}
An EFT is a model that makes accurate predictions within a specific domain. Our thesis is:
\begin{quote}
    \textit{The Golden Algebra is the correct effective theory for the domain of stable mathematical resonances. Its validity is determined by its ability to make sharp, falsifiable predictions that can be tested against observed data.}
\end{quote}

The Golden Algebra functions as a successful physical model. It makes a single, falsifiable prediction: stable resonances must lie on the line `Re(s) = 1/2`.
